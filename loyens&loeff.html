Law firm Loyens & Loeff becomes partner!

The charity division of the well known law firm Loyens & Loeff has decided to support the Newman School project as their Christmas charity project. This new partnership is very exciting for Arun, as it will hopefully help to change the lives of many children.
The next project that Arun would like to sponsor with the help of Loyens & Loeff is a new building for the Newman School in Neotan, the school where Thomas Cramer and Philippe Wijs were based for 2 months. Here a new refectory building is needed to give the children safe and clean eating facilities. The impact of this building would be enormous, as it would mean that the school can meet the requirements to allow hundreds of students to enter the school and give them a good education.
