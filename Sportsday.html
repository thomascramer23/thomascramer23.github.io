Arun sponsors Sports Day at Newman School!

The Newman School in Neotan have just had their Sports day, which was a tremendous success! The two Dutch students on location felt that the students needed a proper sports kit, which we provided.
“The children did not have any sport clothes, which they really needed for physical exercise. Three days of doing sport in their school uniform would not be hygienic and so we wanted them to have proper clothing.” – Philippe Wijs
In response to this need, Arun decided to donate sport kit to all 400 students. “The children were so grateful for having real sport jerseys, the event would not have been the same without it!”
